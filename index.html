Bosses' loot

Here you can see the loot of the bosses. The topmost one is The Machinist's, the middle one is Megalo Don's and the bottommost one is Ringmaster Scarr's.

Medallion: The Machinist's Medallion

It regenerates your sheild over time until 75 sheild.

Mythic: The Machinist's Combat Assault Rifle

It's a quickfiring mythic with the red dot scope and drum mag.

Medallion: Megalo Don's Medallion

It grants you infinite nitro.

Mythics: Megalo Don's Nitro Fists and Megalo Don's Combat Shotgun

The Nitro Fists have five charges that recharges over time as you use them for mobility. You can also use them to punch them, which won't use charges. The Combat Shotgun is a long range shotgun with a drum mag and holo 13 scope.

Medallion: Ringmaster Scarr's Medallion

It grants you infinte ammo and a slight damage buff.

Mythic: Ringmaster's Boom Bolt

It is a crossbow that shoots explosive shots.
